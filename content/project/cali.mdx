[`Cali Institute`](https://www.cali.institute/) is a learning institue website created by[`CeasorCodes`](https://ceasorcodes.vercel.app/)

The Creative Arts Leadership Institute (CALI) is a premier
leadership development organization focused on empowering creative
professionals to lead with vision, purpose, and impact. The CALI
website showcases the programs, events, and resources that CALI
offers to its members and partners, as well as the stories and
achievements of its alumni and faculty.

## Description

The website was developed by me using Nextjs, Tailwindcss, and Framer motion, which are
modern web development tools that enable fast, responsive, and interactive web design. The website reflects the values and
mission of CALI, as well as the creativity and diversity of its community.
