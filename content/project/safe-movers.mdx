## Safe Movers Quote Form

<img
  src="/projects/safemoversform.PNG"
  alt="Safe movers site"
  className=" w-full h-56 rounded-xl"
/>
<span className="mix-blend-plus-lighter text-zinc-200/80">
  Figure 2: Safe Movers Quote Form
</span>

The SafeMovers quote form designed to provide customers with a quick estimate for their moving costs. Developed using the robust Next.js framework and styled with Tailwind CSS and Shadcn, the form offers a sleek, modern interface.
It incorporates React Hook Form for efficient form handling and Zod for input validation, ensuring a smooth user experience.
Upon submission, the form utilizes Resend to securely dispatch the details to the recipient, streamlining the process of obtaining a moving estimate.
